Newton-Raphson Method

The Newton-Raphson Method starts with one initial guess and uses the function and its derivative to find the root.

In this project, the derivative is calculated using the basic polynomial rule: if the term is a x^p, then its derivative is a p x^(p-1).

Formula:
x(n+1) = x(n) - f(x(n)) / f'(x(n))
